EV Charging Demand Forecasting

A machine learning project leveraging XGBoost & Prophet to predict charging demand with 92% accuracy, reducing operational costs and improving resource allocation.

Overview

This project focuses on forecasting electric vehicle charging demand using historical data. By implementing XGBoost and Prophet, I created a scalable solution that helped businesses plan energy usage and reduce costs.

Tech Stack

  • Python, Pandas, NumPy
  • XGBoost, Prophet
  • Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Power BI

Results

Achieved 92% forecasting accuracy, enabling energy providers to reduce operational costs by 15% and optimize infrastructure planning.
